Escapism Portland is a
live action room escape game.


Solve clues, puzzles, and tasks in small teams to unlock new secrets and escape the room! You have only one hour to unravel the mysteries and escape the room. Communication, teamwork, and skills of observation will help the best teams to make it out alive. Escape rooms are the newest craze in entertainment, topping the attractions list for major cities in Europe, Asia, and now the United States.
