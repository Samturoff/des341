Hello! More info on competitive analysis!

Read this article on competitive analysis and SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)

The purpose of this assignment is to expose you to the concept of a competitive analysis. It may be hard to think of some of one or two of the four steps, just do your best. Here’s an example that might play out similarly in your projects:

Let’s say I’m redesigning the site for a brew pub and I choose McMennamin’s as a competitor. A SWOT for McMennamin’s might be:

Strength:
McMennamin’s offers a relaxed, cozy environment that’s easy to bring large groups and friends from out of town to and they have good, locally brewed beer. Their unique hotels also guarantee restaurant customers.

Weakness:
McMennamin’s has a bad reputation for sub-par food and inconsistency in both food and service.

Opportunity:
If McMennamin’s were to step up their food game, and communicated their efforts in improving on this, they could grow their business a great deal.

Threat:
If another brew pub came along that offered the same, cozy, mellow atmosphere, good beer AND good food, they could steal quite a bit of McMennamin’s customer base.
